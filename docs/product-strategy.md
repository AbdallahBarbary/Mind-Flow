# MindFlow Product Strategy

## Product Vision

MindFlow is a calm operating system for thought. It helps people clear mental clutter, capture raw ideas, enter deep focus, and understand their productivity rhythm without making productivity feel noisy or performative.

## Experience Goals

- Give users one obvious next action at every moment.
- Make writing feel private, forgiving, and fast.
- Make focus sessions feel cinematic and intentional.
- Show analytics as reflection, not competition.
- Keep the interface sparse, tactile, and left-aligned.

## Emotional Tone

Peaceful, premium, intentional, distraction-free, emotionally intelligent, and quietly confident.

## Visual Direction

- Pure black background: `#000000`.
- Primary typography: `#FFFFFF`.
- Accent signal: neon blue `#3B82F6`.
- Secondary surfaces: `#101114`, `#171A21`.
- Muted copy: `#9AA3B2`, `#5D6675`.
- Typography pairing: Manrope for cinematic headings, Inter for precise interface text.
- Composition: asymmetrical spacing, large quiet fields, minimal icons, no generic dashboard density.

## Core User Journeys

1. Onboarding introduces calm capture, focused work, and reflective analytics.
2. Auth creates a private user space.
3. Home dashboard surfaces one meaningful focus action and a few calm signals.
4. Brain dump lets the user write without organizing first.
5. Focus mode starts a slow session and saves completion history.
6. Stats summarizes weekly rhythm, streak, and focus totals.
7. Settings and profile keep control quiet and predictable.
